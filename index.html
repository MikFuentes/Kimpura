DISH SPOTLIGHT

Ebi Tempura

A delightful dish that never fails to captivate the taste buds with its exquisite falvors and crispy texture. Made from succulent, plump shrimp, this Japanese delicacy is coated in a light and airy batter before being deep-fried to perfection. Whether enjoyed as an appetizer or a main course, ebi tempura offers a delightful contrast of flavor and textures that is truly irresistable.
